Smart Automatic Pump Controller

The pump controller has stepless pressure regulation intelligent pressure identi cation, suitable for various pressure environment applications of pump control equipment. it can completely replace the traditional System composed with traditional tank, pressure switch, water shortage protection device, check valve, four-way, timer and so on.

Product Technical Parameters:
  • Power: 0.1 -2.2kW Voltage: AC90-260V
  • Maximum Working Pressure: 15 bar
  • Starting pressure setting range: 0-10Bar
  • Maximum Liquid Temperature: 80°C Rated
  • Current: 10A 50/60HZ Joint Screw: G1 "Male
  • Dry run protection timing adjusted to 60 secounds from 200 s
Features:
  • Automatic system for smart control of pump for smart pumps operation control , functioning with both pressure and ow sensors simultaneously.
  • Suitable for all applications of lifting water from lower tanks to upper tanks, or for boosting water in the network, and for water suction from the main supply.
  • Operates silently and without disturbance. The device stops working when the water supply shortage to protect the pump from malfunctions.
  • The device will automatically restart when the water is present again.
  • Equipped with control buttons(+,-) for pressure adjustment. Starting pressure in lifting risers operation is set by + — buttons.
